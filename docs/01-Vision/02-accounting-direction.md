# Business Direction — Accounting & Financial Assistant (Iran)

## Purpose

HiveOS pivots to become an **Enterprise Accounting & Financial Assistant for Iran**. A multi-agent system that understands Iranian accounting standards (based on IFRS), tax laws (Direct Tax, VAT), labor law, social security, auditing, and financial management.

## Target Market

- Iranian businesses (SMEs to enterprises)
- Accounting firms in Iran
- Financial managers and CFOs
- Tax consultants and auditors

## Core Capabilities (Target)

| Domain | Coverage |
|--------|----------|
| Financial Accounting | Iranian GAAP, standards, financial statements |
| Tax Law | Direct Taxes, VAT, withholdings, exemptions |
| Social Security | Regulations, calculations, disputes |
| Labor Law | Contracts, severance, benefits |
| Auditing | Iranian auditing standards, internal audit |
| Management Accounting | Costing, budgeting, financial analysis |
| Practical Scenarios | Real-world case studies, calculations |

## Knowledge Strategy

All knowledge is built incrementally via scheduled research crons. Each session researches one topic, writes structured notes to `docs/06-Research/accounting/`, and marks progress in the index.

## Product Sync

This knowledge base is the **brain** of the future HiveOS Accounting Assistant. Every topic learned → a skill or knowledge file → usable by the agent team.
