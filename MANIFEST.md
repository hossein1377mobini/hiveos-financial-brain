# HiveOS Manifesto

## Our Thesis

The next evolution of AI is not smarter models — it's **coordinated agency**.

A single agent is a Swiss Army knife. A team of agents, orchestrated with purpose, is a factory.

## Core Beliefs

### 1. Agents Are OS Processes
Just as an operating system manages processes, memory, files, and communication between programs, HiveOS manages agents — their lifecycle, context, knowledge, and interactions.

### 2. Workflows Are First-Class Citizens
A flow is not glue code. It's a first-class, declarable, version-controlled artifact that gets defined, tested, and shipped like any other piece of infrastructure.

### 3. Knowledge Lives With The Product
Enterprise products need enterprise-grade memory — not ephemeral chat context. Every decision, every conversation, every architectural choice is documented and traceable.

### 4. Packaging Is Distribution
An agent ecosystem should be as portable as a Docker container. Define once, deploy on any Hermes instance. The package is the unit of distribution.

### 5. Mothership-Satellite Model
One brain can orchestrate many bodies. The Mothership holds the registry, distributes knowledge, routes tasks, and ensures resilience. Satellites execute.

## Our Promise

HiveOS will make building multi-agent systems as natural as writing a single-agent prompt — but with the power, structure, and reliability of an enterprise platform.
